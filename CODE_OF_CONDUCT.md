# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and professional community for the GenAI & LLM Handbook.

## Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on facts, evidence, and clarity.
- Assume good intent; seek to understand before responding.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or exclusionary behavior.

## Scope
- All project spaces: issues, PRs, discussions, chat, and events.

## Reporting
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email: conduct@sans-serif-sentiments.example (replace with a working alias if different).
- Provide links, context, and any relevant details.

## Enforcement
- Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings, issue/PR moderation, or removal from the community spaces.
